The Firm

Lee Sela is a criminal defence lawyer practicing in Toronto, GTA and surrounding areas. She defends people charged with criminal and regulatory offences at trial and on appeal. Lee advocates for people charged with any offence, including fraud, drugs or firearm offences, domestic assault, sexual assault, child pornography, attempted murder and homicides. Lee began her legal career in Ottawa practicing exclusively criminal defence, before moving back home to Toronto and opening her own firm in 2016. Lee has represented clients successfully both at trial and on appeal at all levels of court in Ontario. (ie. Court of Justice, Superior Court of Justice, and Court of Appeal). Lee believes in providing access to justice as well as not crippling you financially during your time of need. She advocates zealously for her clients, yet leaves all judgment for the judge or jury.
